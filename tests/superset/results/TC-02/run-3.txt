### Assessment
This is a textbook AI-generated blog paragraph. Nearly every sentence carries at least one pattern from the catalog. The false concession structure appears twice, the rule of three is present, the opener and closer are both templates, and the piece has no authorial voice. The only judgment call is the "trifecta" label — a human writer might use it, but combined with everything else, it reads as slot-fill.

**Ethics note:** These are signals, not proof. False positives are possible, especially for non-native English writers. The patterns flagged here are statistically more common in LLM output, but a human writing under deadline pressure or in an unfamiliar genre could produce the same shapes. Pair the signal with context.
