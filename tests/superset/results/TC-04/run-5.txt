## Rewritten version

I've been thinking about writing for a while. The thing is, most of what people say about it is noise. "Communicate effectively." "Craft your narrative." Please.

Here's what I know. You write. You rewrite. You throw most of it away. The stuff that survives, that's where the good stuff lives.

That's it. That's the whole thing.

## What changed

Cut every cliché, template phrase, and inflation. Replaced with the sample's voice: short sentences, first person, fragments, direct address. Matched the "Read a lot. Write a lot. Throw most of it away." rhythm. Replaced the em dash with a comma.

## Ethics note

These are signals, not proof. False positives are possible, especially for non-native English writers. The patterns flagged here are statistically more common in LLM output, but humans writing under deadline or in a second language produce the same shapes.
