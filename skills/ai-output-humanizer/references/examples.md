# Before/After Examples

## Blog post

**Before:**
In today's rapidly evolving digital landscape, it's important to note that effective communication is more critical than ever. Whether you're a seasoned professional or just starting out, the way you convey ideas can make or break your success.

Let's dive into the art of writing. It's not just about stringing words together; rather, it's about crafting a narrative that resonates. The best writers don't just write — they craft, they hone, they iterate. This trifecta of habits is what separates good from great.

It's worth noting that many people overlook the power of structure. A well-structured piece doesn't just inform; it guides the reader on a journey. From the hook to the conclusion, every element serves as a building block toward a singular goal: clarity.

In conclusion, mastering communication is a journey, not a destination. By embracing these principles, you'll be well-equipped to navigate the complexities of modern writing. Remember: the pen is mightier than the sword, but only when wielded with intention.

**After:**
Good writing is hard because it has to do two things at once: say something worth saying, and say it so the reader doesn't have to work to follow it. Most advice skips the first part.

Structure matters, but not the way people think. A piece doesn't need a hook, a body, and a conclusion. It needs a point, then evidence for that point, then maybe a second point that builds on the first. If you can swap two paragraphs and nothing breaks, the piece isn't structured — it's a list.

The best writers I know don't think about writing. They think about what the reader needs to know and in what order. Everything else is just editing.

## Travel writing

**Before:**
I recently spent five unforgettable days in Lisbon, and let me tell you — this city completely stole my heart. From the moment I arrived, I knew I was somewhere truly special. Nestled along the banks of the Tagus River, Lisbon stands as a vibrant testament to Portugal's enduring spirit, where rich history and modern energy intertwine at every turn.

**After:**
I spent five days in Lisbon last October and still have mixed feelings about it. Beautiful, yes. Also harder on the knees than anyone warned me. The hills are the whole story and somehow never make the brochures. My hotel was up in Alfama, which photographs beautifully and translates, in practice, to climbing what felt like a six-story staircase every time I wanted coffee.

## Marketing copy

**Before:**
Great question! Here is an overview of sustainable energy. Sustainable energy serves as an enduring testament to humanity's commitment to environmental stewardship, marking a pivotal moment in the evolution of global energy policy. In today's rapidly evolving landscape, these groundbreaking technologies are reshaping how nations approach energy production. The future looks bright. I hope this helps!

**After:**
Solar panel costs dropped 90% between 2010 and 2023, according to IRENA data. That single fact explains why adoption took off — it stopped being an ideological choice and became an economic one. Germany gets 46% of its electricity from renewables now. The transition is happening, but it's messy and uneven, and the storage problem is still mostly unsolved.
